AboutMe

I'm Braxton Joy — A hands-on creative director and multimedia producer with a background in video production, presentation design, branded content, internal communications, and digital storytelling.

Over the years, I have created content that supports executive communication, onboarding and training, campaign visibility, brand consistency, and audience engagement across multiple industries. My experience includes working with cross-functional teams, shaping ideas into clear visual communication, and managing projects from concept through final delivery.

I approach creative work as more than aesthetics. Good creative should clarify, support, and strengthen the message behind it. Whether I am producing video, building a presentation, shaping internal communications, or developing campaign assets, my focus is always the same: create work that looks strong, communicates clearly, and serves a real purpose.

My work is grounded in purpose, discipline, and a commitment to creating with clarity and integrity.
