AboutMe

I'm Braxton Joy — Founder of BraxMov, Creative Director, and Man of Vision Led By Faith.

For over a decade, I've used the power of storytelling to shape brands, inspire audiences, and capture purpose-driven moments on screen. As an award-winning screenwriter and multimedia producer, my mission is to craft visuals that do more than impress — they impact.

BraxMov isn't just a media company. It's a calling. Born out of prayer, perseverance, and God's whisper in a season of stillness, this brand exists to reflect light in a noisy world. Every story I help tell is intentional — rooted in creativity, refined in excellence, and inspired by something bigger than myself.

From Fortune 500 clients to personal brands with powerful missions, I've led productions that blend cinematic beauty with authentic messaging. Whether you're building a brand, launching a vision, or trying to reach hearts — I'm here to help you bring it to life.

Faith. Purpose. Impact.
